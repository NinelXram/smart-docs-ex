# Privacy Policy — Chicken Fill Form

**Last updated:** April 1, 2026

## Overview

Chicken Fill Form is a Chrome extension that helps users create reusable document templates from DOCX and XLSX files with AI-assisted field naming. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Storage

- Your **API key** and **saved templates** are stored locally on your device using `chrome.storage.local`.
- We do **not** operate any servers. No data is sent to or stored on our infrastructure.

## Data Sent to Third Parties

When you use the **field name suggestion** feature, the selected text snippet is sent to the **Google Gemini API** for processing. This data is subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and [Google's API Terms of Service](https://developers.google.com/terms).

No other data is transmitted externally.

## Data Collection

- We do **not** collect, sell, or share any personal data.
- We do **not** use analytics, tracking, or advertising services.
- We do **not** access your browsing history or any website content.

## Permissions

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `storage` | Save your API key and template library locally |
| `unlimitedStorage` | Support large document template binaries |
| `sidePanel` | Display the extension UI in Chrome's side panel |
| `host_permissions` (generativelanguage.googleapis.com) | Communicate with Google Gemini API for field name suggestions |

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
