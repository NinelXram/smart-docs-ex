Privacy Policy — Chicken Fill Form

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Overview

Chicken Fill Form is a Chrome extension that helps users create reusable document templates from DOCX and XLSX files with AI-assisted field naming. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Storage

  • Your API key and saved templates are stored locally on your device using chrome.storage.local.
  • We do not operate any servers. No data is sent to or stored on our infrastructure.

Data Sent to Third Parties

When you use the field name suggestion feature, the selected text snippet is sent to the Google Gemini API for processing. This data is subject to Google's Privacy Policy and Google's API Terms of Service.

No other data is transmitted externally.

Data Collection

  • We do not collect, sell, or share any personal data.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising services.
  • We do not access your browsing history or any website content.
